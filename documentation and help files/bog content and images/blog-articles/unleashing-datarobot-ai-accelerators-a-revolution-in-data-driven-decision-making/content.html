  • Megan Foley
  • / November 16, 2023

In the era of data-driven decision-making, businesses are continually seeking innovative solutions to harness the power of artificial intelligence. Enter DataRobot AI Accelerators, a groundbreaking suite of tools designed to simplify complex tasks and supercharge outcomes. In this blog post, we’ll explore the capabilities of DataRobot AI Accelerators before delving into the strategic partnership between Ready Signal and DataRobot and the unique accelerator born from this collaboration.

Empowering Business Transformation with DataRobot AI Accelerators

The Essence of DataRobot AI Accelerators

DataRobot AI Accelerators stand at the forefront of automated machine learning, providing pre-built solutions tailored to address specific business challenges. These accelerators combine DataRobot’s cutting-edge machine learning platform with domain-specific data and best practices, democratizing the power of AI for organizations without the need for extensive technical expertise.

Versatility in Application

From Hyperparameter Optimization to Data Enriched Time Series analysis, AI Accelerators cover a broad spectrum of use cases. These accelerators are designed to streamline and enhance the machine learning process, allowing businesses to rapidly deploy models and derive actionable insights from their data.

Ease of Use and Accessibility

One of the key advantages of DataRobot AI Accelerators is their accessibility through the ever growing Community Library. They empower users with varying skill sets to leverage the benefits of machine learning without requiring extensive coding or data science knowledge. This democratization of AI fosters a culture of innovation across different departments within an organization.

Performance Metrics and Insights

DataRobot AI Accelerators provide more than just automation; they offer a comprehensive view of model performance and actionable insights. Users can monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of machine learning models through intuitive dashboards, making data-driven decision-making a transparent and informed process.

Explore the AI Accelerators Library

To delve deeper into the diverse applications of DataRobot AI Accelerators and discover how they can address specific business challenges, visit DataRobot’s AI Accelerators Library.

The Synergy of Ready Signal and DataRobot: Elevating Time Series Forecasting with External Data

Strategic Partnership for Innovation

In a strategic collaboration, Ready Signal and DataRobot have combined forces to push the boundaries of what’s possible in time series forecasting. Recognizing the challenges inherent in predicting future trends, demands, and outcomes, the partnership aimed to create an AI Accelerator that seamlessly integrates external data for enhanced forecasting accuracy.

AI Accelerator for Time Series Forecasting with External Data

The result of this collaboration is the AI Accelerator for time series forecasting with external data. This accelerator integrates Ready Signal’s expansive data catalog, offering access to over 500 external data sources (Macro Economic Trends, Consumer Behavior, Weather, Commodity Indexes, and more) into DataRobot’s powerful machine learning platform.

Enhancing Forecasting Capabilities with External Data

By incorporating external signals such as weather, holidays, events, and social media data, businesses can significantly improve the accuracy and reliability of their time series forecasts. This accelerator automates critical steps, including data preparation, feature engineering, model selection, and validation.

Discover the AI Accelerator for Time Series Forecasting

To explore how the AI Accelerator for time series forecasting with external data can transform your forecasting capabilities, visit DataRobot’s AI Accelerators Library.

In conclusion, DataRobot AI Accelerators stand as a beacon of innovation, empowering organizations to leverage the full potential of machine learning. The strategic partnership between Ready Signal and DataRobot further exemplifies the transformative capabilities of AI Accelerators, offering businesses a dynamic solution to optimize their marketing strategies and enhance time series forecasting with external data. Embrace the future of data-driven decision-making with the synergy of Ready Signal and DataRobot.
